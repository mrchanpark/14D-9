Pre-Prod

Engagement & Mobilization

Entirely 🔴 — board-level engagement decisions, privilege establishment. The 10-business-day clock starts when the means to tender are first published, sent or given to holders (commencement under Rule 14d-2), which may coincide with but is not legally identical to the Schedule TO filing date.

flowchart TD
  A(["Tender offer commenced (means to tender first published/sent/given)"]):::red --> B["Engage outside legal counsel"]:::red
  B --> C["Engage financial advisor"]:::red
  C --> D{"Conflicts requiring Special Committee?"}:::redD
  D -- Yes --> E["Form Special Committee & retain independent counsel"]:::red
  D -- No --> F
  E --> F["Establish data room & privilege protocols"]:::red
  F --> G["Calculate 10-business-day deadline from commencement date"]:::yel
  G --> H["Assign production roles & workstreams"]:::red
  H --> I["Establish document version control"]:::yel
  I --> J["Initial board briefing on TO terms & timeline"]:::red
